About Mirade

Mirade McGloin was born in California, but when she was just about one her parents relocated her to Colorado. She spent the rest of her childhood and most of her adult life there. Mirade attended the American Musical and Dramatic Academy for Musical Theatre. Mirade no longer participates in musical theatre but will still enjoy the occasional karaoke session. She spent most of her career in food service serving and then bartending. She took a turn in her career choice and decided to attend the full stack web development bootcamp put on by the University of Denver. She is enjoying her new path and is excited to share her skills with the tech community!
